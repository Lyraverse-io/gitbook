# Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about Lyraverse.

## General Questions

### What is Lyraverse?
Lyraverse is a comprehensive NFT platform built on Hyperliquid, featuring collection creation, multi-phase minting, integrated marketplace, and DAO governance capabilities.

### What blockchain does Lyraverse use?
Lyraverse runs exclusively on **Hyperliquid**, a high-performance L1 blockchain optimized for DeFi and NFTs with low fees and fast transactions.

### What token do I need?
All transactions use **HYPE tokens**. You'll need HYPE for minting, buying, and selling NFTs.

### Are there gas fees?
Network fees are minimal on Hyperliquid (much lower than Ethereum). Platform fees are 2.5% on marketplace sales.

## Wallet & Connection

### Which wallets are supported?
- **MetaMask** (recommended)
- **Coinbase Wallet**
- **Trust Wallet**
- Any Hyperliquid-compatible wallet

### How do I connect my wallet?
1. Install a compatible wallet
2. Configure Hyperliquid network
3. Visit Lyraverse and click "Connect Wallet"
4. Approve the connection

## Creating Collections

### What's the difference between simple and advanced mode?
**Simple Mode**: One public minting phase, perfect for straightforward drops
**Advanced Mode**: Multiple phases, whitelists, custom timing, and DAO integration

### Can I change collection settings after creation?
Some settings are editable (description, links), but core parameters like name, max supply, and creator address are immutable.

### What's the maximum collection size?
10,000 NFTs per collection. Consider user experience and gas costs when choosing your supply.

### How do royalties work?
Creators earn 0-25% of secondary marketplace sales. This incentivizes long-term value creation.

## Minting NFTs

### Why can't I mint?
Common reasons:
- Collection not live yet
- Insufficient HYPE balance
- Exceeded per-wallet limits
- Wrong network selected

### What's the difference between public and whitelist phases?
**Public**: Open to everyone, usually higher price
**Whitelist**: Invite-only, lower price as reward for early supporters

### Can I mint multiple NFTs at once?
Yes, up to 10 NFTs per transaction (subject to collection limits).

### What happens if mint fails?
Funds are automatically returned. Check your balance and try again.

## Marketplace

### How do I buy NFTs?
1. Browse marketplace or collection pages
2. Find an NFT you like
3. Click "Buy Now"
4. Confirm transaction in wallet

### How do I sell NFTs?
1. Go to your NFT's detail page
2. Click "List for Sale"
3. Set your price in HYPE
4. Confirm listing transaction

### What are the fees?
- **Platform Fee**: 2.5% on all sales
- **Royalties**: Creator-set percentage (0-25%)
- **Network**: Minimal Hyperliquid fees

### How long do listings take to appear?
Usually instant, but up to 30 seconds during network congestion.

## DAO & Governance

### What is a DAO on Lyraverse?
A Decentralized Autonomous Organization allows NFT holders to vote on collection decisions, manage treasury, and govern the project.

### How do I create a DAO?
1. Own the collection
2. Go to collection management
3. Click "Create DAO"
4. Configure voting parameters

### What can DAOs do?
- Change collection parameters
- Manage treasury funds
- Approve new mints
- Make governance decisions

## Technical Issues

### Transaction stuck?
- Wait 2-3 minutes (Hyperliquid is fast)
- Check transaction on explorer
- Contact support if truly stuck

### Images not loading?
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check IPFS gateway status

### Wallet not connecting?
- Refresh the page
- Restart wallet extension
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection

## Security & Safety

### Is Lyraverse secure?
- All contracts are verified on Hyperliquid
- No admin keys for user funds
- Regular security audits
- Open-source smart contracts

### How do I protect my NFTs?
- Use hardware wallet for valuable NFTs
- Never share private keys
- Be cautious of phishing attempts
- Verify all transaction details

### What if I lose my wallet?
Your NFTs are permanently lost. Always backup your seed phrase securely and never share it.
