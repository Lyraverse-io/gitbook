# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Lyraverse in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

### Wallet Setup
1. **Install a Web3 Wallet**
   - [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/) (recommended)
   - Coinbase Wallet
   - Any Hyperliquid-compatible wallet

2. **Connect to Hyperliquid Network**
   - Network Name: `Hyperliquid Testnet`
   - RPC URL: Provided by your wallet
   - Chain ID: `998`

3. **Fund Your Wallet**
   - Get HYPE tokens for transactions
   - Use the Hyperliquid faucet for testnet tokens

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit [lyraverse.io](https://lyraverse.io)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top-right corner
3. Select your wallet from the popup
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

## Step 3: Mint Your First NFT

1. **Find a Collection**: Browse to a live collection
2. **Click Mint Tab**: Navigate to the minting interface
3. **Select Quantity**: Choose 1-10 NFTs (based on limits)
4. **Confirm Transaction**: Click "Mint" and approve in wallet
5. **Wait for Confirmation**: ~3-5 seconds on Hyperliquid

## Step 4: Trade on Marketplace

### Buying NFTs
1. **Browse Marketplace**: Find NFTs you like
2. **Click NFT**: View details and price
3. **Buy Now**: Click "Buy for [price] HYPE"
4. **Confirm**: Approve transaction

### Selling NFTs
1. **Go to Your NFT**: Find it in your profile
2. **List for Sale**: Click "List for Sale"
3. **Set Price**: Enter HYPE amount
4. **Confirm**: Approve listing transaction

## Step 5: Create Your Collection

### Simple Mode (Recommended)
1. **Go to Create**: Visit `/create`
2. **Fill Details**:
   - Collection name
   - Description
   - Upload image (1000x1000px recommended)
   - Set price and supply
3. **Deploy**: Click "Create Collection"

### Advanced Mode
1. **Enable Advanced**: Toggle "Advanced Collection"
2. **Configure Phases**: Add public/whitelist phases
3. **Set Dates**: Define start/end times
4. **Customize Rules**: Max per wallet, reserves

## Understanding Key Terms

### Minting Phases
- **Public Phase**: Open to everyone
- **Whitelist Phase**: Invite-only, usually lower price
- **Reserve**: Guaranteed allocation for creator/team
