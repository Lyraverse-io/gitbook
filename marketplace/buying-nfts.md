## Understanding Fees

### Complete Fee Breakdown
```
Listed Price: 10 HYPE
Platform Fee: 0.25 HYPE (2.5%)
Creator Royalty: 0.5 HYPE (5%)
Seller Receives: 9.25 HYPE
Buyer Pays: 10 HYPE + network fees
```

### Fee Categories

#### Platform Fees
- **Fixed Rate**: 2.5% on all marketplace sales
- **Automatic Deduction**: Calculated at purchase
- **Revenue Purpose**: Platform maintenance and development

#### Creator Royalties
- **Variable Rate**: 0-25% set by creator
- **Secondary Sales**: Only on marketplace transactions
- **Creator Revenue**: Supports ongoing development

#### Network Fees
- **Hyperliquid Fee**: Minimal transaction costs
- **Variable Cost**: Depends on network congestion
- **Paid by Buyer**: Added to purchase price
